Who We Are

Born From
the Ocean

Off Peak Surf Bali is Canggu's home for authentic surf experiences. We believe the best sessions happen away from the crowds — at dawn, at dusk, at the moments when the ocean truly belongs to you.

Our team of passionate, certified instructors has helped hundreds of students find their feet on a board. We keep groups small, sessions personal, and the vibes exactly right.
